A little bit about me

I spent my childhood in Japan before moving to the U.S. From Louisiana I moved to the Pacific Northwest, and then after a bit of back and forth I now call Seattle, Washington a home close to my heart. I first pursued a degree in International Studies and Foreign Languages before later earning a degree in Wine Marketing & Sales. My wine journey led me to earn several accolades including becoming a Certified Sommelier with the Court of Master Sommeliers.

As the wine bar I was employed at for 7 years was closing down for good and I passed my Spanish Wine Scholar exam with highest honors, I began to think about my next career move. I wanted to put effort towards something that would ignite my imagination, challenge me and offer me the potential for growth and flexibility. On the one hand, I considered a segeway from Marketing and going into UX/UI design. On the other hand, I've grown to love mobile apps and have a couple ideas of my own. I'm also a very hands-on person and love learning why things work the way they do, so I dove into Software Engineering.
